ZenSearch FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is ZenSearch?

ZenSearch is a browser extension that helps you filter and highlight search results across multiple search engines.

Which search engines are supported?

ZenSearch works with Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Yahoo, Brave Search, and more.

Is ZenSearch free?

Yes, ZenSearch is completely free to use. You can support development through Ko-fi or Afdian if you wish.

Privacy & Security

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. We don’t collect or transmit any personal information.

Does ZenSearch track my searches?

No, ZenSearch does not track your searches or browsing history.

Features & Usage

How many websites can I block/highlight?

There’s no strict limit, but we recommend keeping your lists manageable for better performance.

Can I export my settings?

Yes, you can export your blocked/highlighted websites list and import them on another device.

Does it support regular expressions?

Yes, you can use regular expressions for more advanced filtering.

Troubleshooting

The extension isn’t working

  1. Make sure the extension is enabled
  2. Try refreshing the page
  3. Clear your browser cache

My settings disappeared

Settings are stored locally. If they disappeared, it might be due to:

  • Browser data clearing
  • Extension reinstallation
  • Browser sync issues
