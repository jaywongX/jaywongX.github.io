Privacy Policy

Data Collection

ZenSearch does not collect any personal data. All settings and block/highlight lists are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All your settings are saved in your browser's local storage. This data is not uploaded to any servers.

Permissions

  • Access to web content: Required to implement blocking and highlighting features on search result pages
  • Storage: Required to save your settings and preferences

Third-Party Services

ZenSearch does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
