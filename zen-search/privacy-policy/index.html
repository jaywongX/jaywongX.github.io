ZenSearch Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-02-21

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the ZenSearch browser extension (“we”, “our”, or “the extension”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

The extension collects and stores the following information:

  • Website URLs: URLs that you choose to block or highlight
  • Color Settings: Custom highlight colors you set for specific websites
  • Extension Settings: Your preferences for the extension’s functionality
  • Language Preference: Your selected interface language

All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage (chrome.storage.local). We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely to:

  • Filter and highlight search results according to your preferences
  • Maintain your customized settings
  • Provide the core functionality of the extension

Data Storage

All data is:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Only accessible by the extension
  • Removable by uninstalling the extension

Third-Party Access

The extension:

  • Does not share any data with third parties
  • Does not use external analytics services
  • Does not include any tracking mechanisms

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • storage: To save your settings locally
  • activeTab: To modify search results on search engine pages
  • scripting: To apply filters to search results
  • contextMenus: To provide right-click menu options

Changes to Search Results

The extension only modifies the visual presentation of search results by:

  • Hiding results from blocked websites
  • Highlighting results from marked websites
  • These modifications are temporary and only visible to you

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View all stored data through the extension’s settings
  • Delete all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Modify or remove individual settings at any time

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date at the top of this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please create an issue on our GitHub repository.
