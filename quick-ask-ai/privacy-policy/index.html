Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Quick Ask AI does not collect any personal data. All settings and configurations are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

Your settings, including:

  • AI assistant configurations
  • Ask mode settings
  • Keyboard shortcuts
  • Tab behavior preferences

are all saved in your browser's local storage. This data is not uploaded to any servers.

Permissions

  • Access to web content: Required to detect selected text and handle right-click events
  • Storage: Required to save your settings and preferences
  • Tabs: Required to open AI assistant websites in new tabs
  • Context Menus: Required to add menu items to the right-click menu

Third-Party Services

Quick Ask AI does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. When you use the extension to ask questions:

  • Your selected text is sent directly to your chosen AI assistant's website
  • We do not intercept, store, or process this data in any way
  • The privacy policies of the AI assistant services you use will apply to your interactions with them

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
